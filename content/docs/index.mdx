# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 30, 2025**

## Introduction

Thank you for using Phoenix Earthquake App ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Location Information
We collect approximate location data to determine which country you are in. This allows us to send you relevant earthquake notifications for your region (Japan, Taiwan, or Indonesia). We do not track your precise location.

### Device Information
We collect only the minimal device information required to send you push notifications. We do not collect any other sort of informations.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Send you relevant earthquake and tsunami alerts based on your country
- Improve and optimize the App's functionality

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:
- Service providers who help us deliver our services
- If required by law or to protect our rights

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

## Your Choices

You can disable location permissions through your device settings, but this may limit your ability to receive relevant alerts.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website.
